Unserra is a 2D Zelda-like on pixel art, made on few months with a team of 9 students.

You embody Izel, girl banished from her tribe who discovers a strange weapon allowing her to inhale the life and to restore it in the flora and fauna. Choose what what you will make of this weapon, destruction or life?
